Our Games

As the maintainers and guardians of our beloved Flame Engine, the Blue Fire team has dabbled in making games using Flame in the past.

These are some of the games we've made, all in different levels of completeness and polish:

Main Games

  • Gravity Runner, reverse gravity and avoid obstacles in this challenging endless runner
  • BGUG, Break Guns Using Gems, a fun endless runner with a twist
  • Bob Builder, an addictive casual game to pass the time
  • Tales of a Lost Mine, an unfinished Metriodvania side-scroller
  • Snake Chef, classic Snake meets Overcooked

Game Jams

These games were entries to various Game Jams that we participated:

  • Heeve, an RTS where you play the aliens - our entry for GitHub's GameOff 2021
  • Ghost vs Wonderland, fight your way through Wonderland - our unofficial entry for the Flame Game Jam
